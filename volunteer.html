Volunteer

Across the United States 70% of all firefighters are volunteers, protecting their communities as first responders in times of emergency. In Connecticut, nearly 85% of all firefighters are volunteers continuing a long-standing tradition of community driven public service.

Plainfield Fire Co. #1 is actively seeking volunteers! If you think you have what it takes, fill out a membership application and return it in-person to the station any Wednesday evening between 6:00pm - 9:00pm.
