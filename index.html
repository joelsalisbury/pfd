Plainfield Fire Department

About Us

The Plainfield Fire Company #1 is a dedicated volunteer organization serving our residents through fire suppression and prevention, emergency medical response, and exceptional community service. We are committed to providing a superior level of public safety that continually improves the quality of life, health and wellbeing of the people who live in and visit Plainfield.

The Town of Plainfield is 42 square miles and is home to approximately 16,000 residents. Plainfield is broken into 4 villages, each served by their own fire department. Ambulance transport is handled by an ambulance corps independent of the fire departments.

  • Fire Co. #1
  • Moosup Fire Company
  • Central Village Fire Co. # 1
  • Atwood Hose Fire Co. (Wauregan)
  • American Legion Post 91 Ambulance Fund

911 call receipt and emergency dispatch is performed Quinebaug Valley Emergency Communications, Inc. located in Killingly, Connecticut.

The Plainfield Fire Co #1 serves the Plainfield Fire District in the Town of Plainfield. Our primary service area is approximately 25 square miles.
